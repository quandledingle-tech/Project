Sustainable Materials

We believe that beautiful furniture begins with responsible material selection. Our bedroom collection features wood sourced exclusively from FSC-certified forests, ensuring sustainable harvesting practices.

Natural finishes are applied by hand using non-toxic, water-based formulations that highlight the inherent beauty of the wood while protecting indoor air quality in your home.

Hardware elements are crafted from recycled brass and steel, combining durability with environmental consciousness.
