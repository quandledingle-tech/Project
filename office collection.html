The Science of Comfort

At LUX, we understand that productivity and well-being are intimately connected. Our office collection incorporates the latest research in ergonomics and human factors engineering to support healthy work habits.

Each piece is tested extensively to ensure proper positioning, reduce strain, and promote movement throughout the workday. We collaborate with occupational health specialists to validate our designs and refine our approach to workplace furniture.

The result is a collection that not only looks beautiful but actively contributes to your physical comfort and mental focus.
